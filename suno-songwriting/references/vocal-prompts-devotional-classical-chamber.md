# Devotional, Classical, and Chamber Vocal Prompts

VP037-VP042: sustained tone, modal gravity, choir/liturgical behavior, and formal restraint.

### VP037 — Drone Lotus Baritone
- **Short voice identity phrase:** Meditative modal baritone with devotional breath arcs.
- **Full Suno style prompt:** baritone devotional lead with dark smooth resonance, clear breath-supported phonation, long sustained phrases, sparse slow vibrato, legato vowel-centered diction, and restrained modal slides. Devotional ambient/classical-fusion arrangement with drone bed, spacious hand percussion, and meditative atmosphere. Intro should feel like a drone invocation, Verse 1 should be calm and low, pre-chorus should rise patiently, chorus should open into prayerful sustained vowels, bridge should be nearly still, breakdown should repeat like mantra, final chorus should reach a prayerful peak. Emphasize breath control and stillness. Avoid parody sacred style, over-ornamenting, and static lifeless pacing.
- **Negative prompt / avoid list:** named devotional singer, fake language, excessive melisma, muddy drone, generic world music.
- **Suggested Suno tags:** [Drone Invocation], [Tala Arrival], [Prayerful Peak], [Ecstatic Repetition], [Ritual Close]
- **Expected audible result:** A slow-building spiritual reading where minimal movement feels intense.
- **Comparison notes:** Compare against VP039 for faster ecstatic spiral and VP058 for folk-drone hybrid.

### VP038 — Chapel Glass Mezzo
- **Short voice identity phrase:** Pure chamber mezzo with chapel clarity and restrained vibrato.
- **Full Suno style prompt:** mezzo chamber voice with clear glass timbre, pure clean phonation, balanced mix/head register, delayed narrow choral vibrato, precise soft diction, and legato phrasing. Chamber-pop/classical crossover arrangement with chapel room, piano/strings, and restrained harmonies. Verse 1 should be intimate and pure, pre-chorus should rise in an arch, chorus should glow rather than belt, bridge should be exposed and reverent, breakdown should remain delicate but pulsed, final chorus should add layered chamber harmonies. Emphasize purity, space, and word clarity. Avoid operatic excess, sterile emotion, and heavy pop tuning.
- **Negative prompt / avoid list:** excessive vibrato, stiff diction, overlarge reverb, bland choir pad.
- **Suggested Suno tags:** [Ma Space], [Layered Harmonies], [Prayerful Peak], [Afterglow], [Resolved Cadence]
- **Expected audible result:** A transparent chamber version with exposed vocal control.
- **Comparison notes:** Compare against VP040 for higher madrigal silver and VP011 for countertenor ceremonial distance.

### VP039 — Qawwali Spiral Lead
- **Short voice identity phrase:** Ecstatic devotional fusion lead with spiraling repetition.
- **Full Suno style prompt:** devotional call lead with bright fervent timbre, energized clear phonation, forceful chest-mix calls, fast ornamental vibrato, spiraling melisma, repeated phrases, and precise syllabic diction. Qawwali-inspired world-soul fusion arrangement with handclaps, harmonium-like color, percussion, and group response. Verse 1 should begin restrained, pre-chorus should intensify through repetition, chorus should open fervently, bridge should become prayerful and exposed, breakdown should repeat the chant with growing urgency, final chorus should reach ecstatic call-and-response. Emphasize spiritual escalation and controlled ornament. Avoid imitating real singers, caricature accent, non-English fake chanting, and obscuring lyrics with runs.
- **Negative prompt / avoid list:** named qawwali singer, fake sacred text, excessive melisma, parody accent, chaotic group vocals.
- **Suggested Suno tags:** [Qawwali-Style Climax], [Ecstatic Repetition], [Group Response], [Melismatic Lift], [Devotional Peak]
- **Expected audible result:** A high-intensity devotional-fusion version where repetition creates lift.
- **Comparison notes:** Compare against VP014 for gospel-soul peak and VP037 for stiller devotional baritone.

### VP040 — Madrigal Silver Soprano

- **Full Suno style prompt:** soprano chamber voice with silver focused timbre, clean non-breathy phonation, straight tone with tiny phrase-end shimmer, precise chamber diction, and subtle word-painting turns. ### VP042 — Oratorio Ember Tenor
- **Short voice identity phrase:** Heroic classical-crossover tenor with ember warmth.
- **Full Suno style prompt:** tenor classical-crossover voice with ember-warm ringing timbre, supported clear phonation, projected upper mix/head blend, wide controlled vibrato, formal diction, and long arching lines. Cinematic oratorio-pop arrangement with orchestral lift, choir-like backing, and dramatic percussion. Verse 1 should begin restrained and noble, pre-chorus should expand through breath, chorus should open heroically on sustained vowels, bridge should be prayerful and exposed, breakdown should become rhythmic but dignified, final chorus should surge with orchestral harmonies. Emphasize support and noble yearning. Avoid excessive opera parody, swallowed consonants, and constant fortissimo.
- **Negative prompt / avoid list:** named tenor, operatic caricature, too much vibrato, muddy choir, pop autotune.
- **Suggested Suno tags:** [Grand Overture], [Orchestral Lift Over Trap Drums], [Anthemic Peak], [Prayerful Peak], [Final Surge]
- **Expected audible result:** A heroic, formal reading with strong breath support and cinematic scale.
- **Comparison notes:** Compare against VP010 for theater belt and VP052 for soprano metal-cinematic scale.
