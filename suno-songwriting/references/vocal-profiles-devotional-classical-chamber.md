- **Distinguishing features:** Meditative modal phrasing and breath control.

- **Likely weaknesses/failure modes:** Can become too static; ornament must stay restrained.
- **Best genres:** Devotional fusion, ambient pop, classical crossover, world-chamber pop.

- **Suno-relevant style vocabulary:** devotional baritone, drone invocation, modal slides, prayerful peak, long breath arcs.
- **What to listen for:** Whether stillness becomes intensity rather than passivity.
