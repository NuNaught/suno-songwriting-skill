# Hip-Hop and Urban Beat Genres

Use for rap-adjacent beat architecture, MC-driven delivery, 808s, sparse loops, urban dance grooves, and club chants.

| Genre name | Summary | Key identifying characteristics | Associated instruments |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Trap (hip-hop)                             | Modern rap production style rooted in Southern U.S. hip-hop.                            | 808 sub-bass, rapid hi-hat rolls, sparse dark harmony, rhythmic vocal delivery.                                      | Drum machine, sampler, synth bass, voice                                    |
| Reggaetón                                  | Urban Caribbean-Latin popular genre with strong dance emphasis.                         | Dembow rhythm, repetitive hooks, rap-sung delivery, club-oriented production.                                        | Drum programming, synths, sampler, voice                                    |
| Dembow                                     | Dominican dance music centered on an aggressive looped rhythmic cell.                   | Sparse beat architecture, chant-like vocals, repetitive groove, raw party energy.                                    | Drum programming, sampler, synths, voice                                    |
| Baile funk                                 | Brazilian favela-based electronic dance style.                                          | Heavy percussion loops, MC-driven flow, raw bass, call-and-response energy.                                          | Drum machines, samplers, voice                                              |
| Jersey club                                | Fast East Coast club music built for dance battles and chopped samples.                 | Kick triplets, bed squeaks/chops, short loop structures, high-energy tempo.                                          | Sampler, drum programming, voice chops                                      |
| Drill                                      | Dark, rhythmically tense rap style with strong mainstream visibility.                   | Sliding bass, sparse ominous beats, clipped flows, menacing mood.                                                    | Voice, drum programming, synth bass, sampler                                |
| Lo-fi hip-hop                              | Relaxed beat-based style that became a major streaming and internet genre.              | Dusty samples, soft swing, jazzy loops, subdued textures, low-intensity groove.                                      | Sampler, drum programming, electric piano, vinyl-texture processing         |
