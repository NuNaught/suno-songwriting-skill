# Groove and Dance-Call Vocal Prompts

VP031-VP036: clave, dembow, Afro-diasporic pocket, carnival scale, and call-response leadership.

### VP031 — Dembow Silk Tenor
- **Short voice identity phrase:** Silky dance tenor with dembow-adjacent rhythmic diction.
- **Full Suno style prompt:** tenor dance lead with silky bright-warm timbre, clear lightly nasal phonation, smooth mix, minimal vibrato, percussive consonants, liquid vowels, and dembow-adjacent rap-sung syncopation. Latin club pop/dancehall-pop arrangement with glossy percussion, warm bass, and restrained tropical color. Verse 1 should feel intimate but rhythmic, pre-chorus should lift smoothly, chorus should glide over the groove, bridge should soften to clean tone, breakdown should become danceable chant, final chorus should add callouts and harmony. Emphasize pocket and warmth. Avoid caricature accent, oversexualized delivery, and monotonous hook repeats.
- **Negative prompt / avoid list:** exaggerated dialect, mumbled rap-singing, generic club drop, overdone autotune.
- **Suggested Suno tags:** [Dembow Entry], [Locked Groove], [Chanted Hook], [Dancefloor Peak], [Ad-Lib Finale]
- **Expected audible result:** A smooth dance interpretation centered on rhythmic diction and groove.
- **Comparison notes:** Compare against VP032 for clave phrasing and VP060 for festival-scale dance delivery.

### VP032 — Clave Copper Alto
- **Short voice identity phrase:** Copper alto with clave-aware pop articulation.
- **Full Suno style prompt:** alto voice with copper bright-warm timbre, forward clear phonation, crisp consonants, open vowels, brief warm vibrato, and clave-conscious syncopated phrasing. Latin pop/son-inspired crossover arrangement with clave-set percussion, polished bass, and possible coro response. Verse 1 should stay clear and groove-aware, pre-chorus should rise with rhythmic tension, chorus should open passionately, bridge should reduce to voice and pulse, breakdown should become coro/pregón-like call-response, final chorus should add bright group answers. Emphasize clave placement and diction. Avoid exaggerated accent, overbusy percussion, and losing the downbeat.
- **Negative prompt / avoid list:** named Latin singer, caricature diction, cluttered percussion, unclear English, generic salsa loop.
- **Suggested Suno tags:** [Clave Set], [Montuno Launch], [Coro/Pregón], [Big Chorus], [Call and Response]
- **Expected audible result:** A rhythmically articulated version where phrase placement differs from straight pop.
- **Comparison notes:** Compare against VP031 for dembow flow and VP056 for tango rubato drama.
